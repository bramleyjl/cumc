Worship

All are welcome at Clinton United Methodist Church. Our worship is a blended service with praise music as well as traditional hymns and liturgy. Our music leader, organist/pianist and band lead us in engaging music. Our choir provides thoughtful musical reflections and we are blessed throughout the year by gifted musicians from our church and community. The message is presented in the form of a series on a particular theme as well as from weekly scripture readings that are part of the lectionary. Communion is open to all and served on the first Sunday of each month. You can expect the service to last about an hour.

Our church is accessible to the handicapped with plenty of free parking. Large print hymnals and bulletins are available for the visually impaired, and pocket radio receivers are available for those people requiring hearing assistance.
